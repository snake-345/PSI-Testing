Your Nissan Dealership in Medford, MA

Welcome to Colonial Nissan, your source for Nissan models in Medford. We have all the most popular Nissan vehicles including SUVs and crossovers like the ever-popular Rogue and Rogue Sport along with the Kicks, Pathfinder, Murano, and more. We have sedan options too including the Altima, Maxima, and Sentra. If you are looking for a truck or even a commercial vehicle, then Colonial Nissan can even help you there with our selection of NV cargo vans along with Titan, Titan XD, and Frontier trucks. You can always count on us, and we can provide anything you are looking for from new Nissan models to used vehicles, and even Nissan service, this team is here for you. 

The Colonial auto group has been operating since 1992, and in that time, we have continued to strive for excellence as we grew from one dealership into many. Now we employ over 700 people in Massachusetts and sell over 20,000 cars annually. Colonial Nissan is one more extension of our fine auto group, and with it we are proud to serve fine Nissan vehicles to Medford and all the surrounding areas. We have satisfied customers in Cambridge, Malden, Arlington, and all throughout Greater Boston, and we welcome you to be the next satisfied customer.

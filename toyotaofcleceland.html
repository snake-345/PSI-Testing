Toyota Dealership near Chattanooga,  TN

Toyota of Cleveland has been providing an impressive selection of Toyota vehicles to the area for over 25 years. From our Chattanooga location, we are able to serve a wide area, including Cleveland, Chattanooga, Collegedale, Dalton, and more.

Toyota of Cleveland offers an extensive inventory of new and used vehicles. Our new inventory includes all of the popular models from the Toyota lineup. This includes iconic cars like the Corolla and Camry that deliver efficiency and comfort, along with versatile crossovers and SUVs like the RAV4, Highlander, and 4Runner and workhorse pickup trucks like the Tacoma and Tundra.

Toyota of Cleveland provides a variety of services to our customers. Our service center and body shop are here to help with routine maintenance along with any more substantial repairs. The Toyota of Cleveland website is here to serve as a resource for car shoppers and owners. Take a look around to learn more about the vehicles and services we have to offer. Get in touch with our team if you have any questions or comments.
